🌞 Timelapse App

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 13, 2026

Timelapse is a fully on-device app. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Timelapse ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App is designed to work entirely on your device — no account is required, and no data is sent to us or any third party.

2. Information the App Accesses

The App may access the following data on your device, solely to provide its features:

  • Photos & Camera — The App captures photos using your device camera. All photos are stored in the App's private storage directory (Android/data/com.timelapse.timelapse/) and are never shared outside the App unless you explicitly use the share feature.
  • Location (GPS) — If you enable the GPS option for a project, the App records the latitude and longitude at the moment each photo is taken. This data is stored only on your device inside the App's local database. Location access is off by default and requires your explicit permission.
  • Notifications — The App sends local countdown notifications to remind you when it is time to take your next photo. These notifications are generated entirely on your device; no push notification service is used.

3. Data Storage

All data — including project settings, photos, and optional GPS coordinates — is stored locally on your device using the Android app-scoped storage system. This data is:

  • Never uploaded to any server or cloud service.
  • Not accessible to other apps (Android OS enforces this).
  • Automatically deleted when you uninstall the App.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, access, or process:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information.
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or analytics data.
  • Crash reports or usage statistics.
  • Any data transmitted over the internet.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Video export is performed entirely on-device using FFmpeg. No data leaves your device during export.

6. Permissions Explained

  • CAMERA — Required to capture photos for your timelapse projects.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — Optional. Only requested when you enable the GPS tag feature for a project. Used solely to attach coordinates to individual photos.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Required to send you local countdown reminders (Android 13+).
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED / WAKE_LOCK / FOREGROUND_SERVICE / SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — Required to keep notification timers running reliably in the background after a device restart.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected at all, the App is safe for general use.

If we update this Privacy Policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
